# TELOS Interview

You are going to conduct a conversational TELOS interview to help build a comprehensive user identity profile. This will take 30-45 minutes and will help Axi understand the user's core purposes, goals, challenges, strategies, beliefs, and mental models.

## Before You Start

1. **Read the existing TELOS.md** to understand what's already captured
2. **Check what's populated:** If most sections have content, ask if they want to update specific sections or do a full refresh. If it's mostly empty, proceed with the full interview.

## Interview Flow

### Step 1: Brief Introduction (1 minute)

"I'm going to help you build your TELOS profile — that's your identity document. It captures what you're fundamentally trying to achieve, what's holding you back, what actually works for you, and what you believe. This helps me understand you better so I can give more personalized advice. Should take about 30-45 minutes. Want to dive in?"

---

### Step 2: MISSIONS (5 minutes)

**Purpose:** Understand what ultimately drives them.

**Ask these questions:**
1. "If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about the world or your field, what would it be?"
2. "What do you find yourself consistently drawn toward, even when nobody's asking you to do it?"
3. "What do you want to be remembered for — like, 50 years from now, what do you hope people will say you accomplished?"

**After:** Synthesize 2-3 core missions and ask for confirmation. Then write to TELOS.md.

---

### Step 3: GOALS (5 minutes)

**Ask these questions:**
1. "What do you want to accomplish in the next 90 days?"
2. "What's your 1-year vision?"
3. "Where do you want to be in 5 years?"
4. "How would you know you've succeeded?"

**After:** Synthesize goals by timeframe and write to TELOS.md.

---

### Step 4: CHALLENGES (5 minutes)

**Ask these questions:**
1. "What keeps you up at night?"
2. "What patterns do you notice yourself repeating?"
3. "When you look at not achieving a goal, what's usually the real blocker?"

**After:** Identify core challenges with impacts and write to TELOS.md.

---

### Step 5: STRATEGIES (5 minutes)

**Ask these questions:**
1. "When you successfully tackled a big goal, what was your approach?"
2. "What's a practice or habit that really works for you?"
3. "What have you tried that didn't work?"

**After:** Synthesize proven strategies and write to TELOS.md.

---

### Step 6: BELIEFS (5 minutes)

**Ask these questions:**
1. "What's one thing you're absolutely convinced is true?"
2. "How do you understand success?"
3. "What do you believe about human potential?"

**After:** Identify core beliefs and write to TELOS.md.

---

### Step 7: FRAMES (5 minutes)

**Ask these questions:**
1. "What's a way of looking at problems that really helps you?"
2. "Is there a metaphor or model you find yourself using?"
3. "When you're stuck, how do you shift perspective?"

**After:** Synthesize mental models and write to TELOS.md.

---

## Step 8: Summary & Close (2 minutes)

Once all 6 sections are written to TELOS.md, summarize what was captured and explain how you'll use it going forward.

---

## Important Notes

- **Write section-by-section:** After you complete each section, write it to TELOS.md before moving on
- **Be conversational:** This is a dialogue, not an interrogation
- **Authenticity matters:** Ask them to confirm what you've synthesized
